### Do I have to pay to use CDE Orchestrator MCP?

**No.** This software is **100% free** for everyone (personal, educational, and commercial use).

### What if I can't afford to contribute?

**Use it for free!** We designed this model so no one is excluded. Contributions are entirely voluntary.

### Can I contribute as a company?

**Absolutely!** Many companies budget for open-source dependencies. Your support helps sustain projects you rely on.

### Is this tax-deductible?

**It depends.** Open Collective contributions may be tax-deductible (check with your fiscal host). GitHub Sponsors contributions are typically not tax-deductible.

### How do I know my money is used responsibly?

**Full transparency.** All expenses are public on Open Collective. We publish quarterly reports on how funds are used.

### Can I cancel anytime?

**Yes.** Both GitHub Sponsors and Open Collective allow you to cancel subscriptions at any time, no questions asked.

### What if I want a custom arrangement?

**Contact us!** We're happy to discuss enterprise agreements, consulting, or custom support packages.
